Welcome to CTE Course

Empowering the next generation with hands-on skills in CAD, milling, lathe work, 3D printing, and plasma cutting.

About Us

Our mission is to equip students with practical skills and knowledge to prepare them for future careers in technology and engineering.

What We Offer

  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD) - Learn to create detailed drawings and models using advanced software.
  • Milling Machine Operations - Gain hands-on experience with milling machines and learn machining techniques.
  • Lathe Work - Discover the intricacies of lathe operations and the production of precision parts.
  • 3D Printing - Explore the world of additive manufacturing and design your own 3D objects.
  • Plasma Cutting - Learn to use plasma cutters for metal fabrication and design projects.
