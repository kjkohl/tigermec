Welcome to CTE Course

Empowering the next generation with hands-on skills in CAD, milling, lathe work, 3D printing, and plasma cutting.

About Us

Our mission is to equip students with practical skills and knowledge to prepare them for future careers in technology and engineering. We focus on innovative teaching methods and hands-on learning experiences to inspire creativity and critical thinking.

What We Offer

  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD) - Create detailed drawings and models with advanced software.
  • Milling Machine Operations - Gain hands-on experience with machining techniques.
  • Lathe Work - Discover lathe operations for precision parts production.
  • 3D Printing - Explore additive manufacturing and design your own 3D objects.
  • Plasma Cutting - Use plasma cutters for metal fabrication projects.
