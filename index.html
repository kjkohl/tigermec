About Us

Our mission is to provide students with practical skills and knowledge that prepare them for their future careers in technology and engineering. We focus on innovative teaching methods and hands-on learning experiences to inspire creativity and critical thinking.

At the Tiger MEC, we emphasize teamwork, safety, and real-world applications of skills in manufacturing and design. Our instructors bring a wealth of industry experience, ensuring students gain insights that will benefit their careers.
