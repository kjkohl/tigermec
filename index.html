Welcome to CTE Course

At the Massillon CTE Course, we empower the next generation with hands-on skills in various fields of manufacturing and engineering.

Join us to explore:

  • Advanced Computer-Aided Design (CAD)
  • Precision Milling and Lathe Operations
  • 3D Printing Innovations
  • Metal Fabrication Techniques with Plasma Cutting

About Us

Our mission is to equip students with practical skills and knowledge that will prepare them for successful careers in technology and engineering.

We employ innovative teaching methods and hands-on learning experiences to inspire creativity and critical thinking.

Our dedicated instructor ensures that every student has the opportunity to excel in a safe and supportive environment.

What We Offer

  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD): Learn to create detailed drawings and models using industry-standard software. This course emphasizes design principles and technical drawings.
  • Milling Machine Operations: Gain hands-on experience with milling machines, learning essential machining techniques for precise manufacturing.
  • Lathe Work: Discover the intricacies of lathe operations and the production of precision parts, focusing on accuracy and safety.
  • 3D Printing: Explore additive manufacturing and the design of 3D objects. Students will learn software and techniques for prototyping.
  • Plasma Cutting: Learn to use plasma cutters for metal fabrication and artistic projects. Emphasis is placed on safety and creative design.

These courses are designed to provide in-depth knowledge and practical skills, preparing students for various careers in the manufacturing and design fields.
