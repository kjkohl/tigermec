Welcome!

Empowering the next generation with hands-on skills in CAD, milling, lathe work, 3D printing, and plasma cutting.

About Us

Our mission is to equip students with practical skills and knowledge to prepare them for future careers in technology and engineering. We focus on innovative teaching methods and hands-on learning experiences to inspire creativity and critical thinking.

Our dedicated instructor ensures that every student has the opportunity to excel in a safe and supportive environment.

What We Offer

  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD) - Learn to create detailed drawings and models using advanced software.
  • Milling Machine Operations - Gain hands-on experience with milling machines and learn machining techniques.
  • Lathe Work - Discover the intricacies of lathe operations and the production of precision parts.
  • 3D Printing - Explore the world of additive manufacturing and design your own 3D objects.
  • Plasma Cutting - Learn to use plasma cutters for metal fabrication and design projects.

These are designed to provide in-depth knowledge and practical skills in a supportive environment, preparing students for various careers in the manufacturing and design fields.
